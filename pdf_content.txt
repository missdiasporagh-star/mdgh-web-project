Introduction
Nubian Crown Company Limited is a pioneering organization based in Ghana, founded by a passionate
former beauty pageant contestant dedicated to merging beauty with social impact. This innovative
company is behind the Miss Diaspora Ghana pageant, which seeks to celebrate and empower women
of African descent living abroad while fostering a strong connection to their roots.
The founder's vision for Nubian Crown Limited is to create a platform where beauty and social work
intersect, promoting initiatives that uplift communities and address various social challenges. By
leveraging the influence of pageantry, the company encourages participants to engage in community
service and advocate for meaningful change.
Through Miss Diaspora Ghana, Nubian Crown Limited not only highlights the beauty and achievements
of its contestants but also emphasizes the importance of social responsibility. The organization aims to
inspire women to use their platforms for good, ultimately benefiting the community and contributing
to the overall development of society.
We are pleased to introduce the maiden edition of Miss Diaspora Ghana, an empowering cultural
initiative designed to celebrate the beauty, intellect, and heritage of young African women living across
the globe. This pageant is more than a showcase of eleganceÔÇöit is a movement that bridges Ghana
and its vibrant diaspora, using culture, advocacy, and community engagement as a unifying platform.
Miss Diaspora Ghana seeks to highlight the invaluable role that Africans abroad play in national
development. By bringing together talented and ambitious young women from the diaspora, the
pageant promotes cultural pride, encourages investment in Ghana, and provides a springboard for
leadership, entrepreneurship, and social impact. The event also seeks to support national priorities
such as youth empowerment, tourism promotion, and the continuation of the "Beyond the Return"
agenda.
Your support as a sponsor will not only enable the success of this prestigious event but also position
your organization as a champion of culture, inclusion, and womenÔÇÖs empowerment on both a local and
international scale.
The Miss Diaspora Beauty Pageant aims to create a unique platform for black women in the diaspora to
connect with their roots while celebrating beauty, culture and social responsibility. Hosted in Ghana,

3. Program Outline
The Miss Diaspora Ghana pageant is structured to deliver maximum cultural impact, contestant
development, and audience engagement through the following phases:
´éÀ International Auditions & Screening
A virtual preliminary selections held in segmented diaspora cities such as Atlanta and New York
to identify and shortlist outstanding African / African American women from the global
community.
´éÀ Virtual Training & Grooming Sessions
Shortlisted contestants undergo a series of mentorship workshops focused on public speaking,
personal branding, Ghanaian culture, current affairs, and leadership development.
´éÀ Cultural Immersion Week in Ghana
Finalists will participate in a week-long experience including cultural tours, understanding the
ways of life of Ghanaians, community service, media engagements, and sessions with mentors
and social impact leaders in Ghana before the grand finale.
´éÀ Grand Finale Event
A professionally produced, televised finale in Accra featuring talent showcases, fashion
segments, question-and-answer rounds, and the crowning of Miss Diaspora Ghana. The event
will be attended by dignitaries, media, and cultural stakeholders.
´éÀ Post-Event Ambassadorship
The crowned queen and runners-up will take on ambassadorial roles for various causes such as
youth development, tourism promotion, womenÔÇÖs empowerment, and diaspora unity
throughout their 1-year reign.
